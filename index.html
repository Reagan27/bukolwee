Proudly Bukolwe Alumni The Bukolwe Alumni Network (Class of 2015) is a dedicated platform for past students of Bukolwe Secondary School to reconnect, network, and contribute to the legacy of our alma mater. Our mission is to strengthen bonds, support career growth, and give back to the school that shaped us.

Through this platform, members of the Class of 2015 can stay in touch, share success stories, engage in mentorship programs, and participate in alumni events. Whether you're looking to reconnect with old classmates, explore career opportunities, or support school initiatives, this is your home for all things Bukolwe.
